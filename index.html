Who We Are

We are a team of three students taking the Introduction to Machine Learning course at AUB. Our project explores using machine learning to transition songs between genres through actionable recommendations.

Our Project

"Let's Groove with Mozart," bridges the gap between music genres using machine learning. By starting with genre classification across up to four distinct styles, we lay the groundwork for actionable recommendations that help transform classical songs into disco. This process allows musicians to adapt timeless works for new audiences without compromising their creative integrity. Beyond transformation, our recommendation system suggests appropriate songs for remixing to expand a musician's creative possibilities.
